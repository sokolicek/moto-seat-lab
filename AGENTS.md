# AGENTS.md

This is a concept-stage content project for a future motorcycle seat comfort website.

## Working Rules

- Do not start implementation code until the concept and content model are accepted.
- Work plan-first.
- Keep content modular and reusable across countries, languages, and motorcycle models.
- Prefer documented assumptions over hidden guesses.
- Mark facts that require research.
- Separate opinion, experience, and verified information.
- Do not add affiliate links without a disclosure strategy.
- Do not present safety-critical DIY work as risk-free.
- Include warnings where seat modifications can affect control, posture, wiring, waterproofing, heating, or warranty.

## Human Approval Gates

Ask for approval before:

- choosing a website stack,
- creating the first website code,
- adding paid tools,
- choosing affiliate programs,
- publishing content publicly,
- making strong claims about medical or ergonomic outcomes,
- recommending product rankings as final.

## Standard Workflow

1. Orchestrator identifies the next content/design decision.
2. Spec Writer turns the idea into clear content requirements.
3. Critic finds gaps, risks, and overcomplication.
4. Researcher lists facts that need verification.
5. Content Architect updates the structure.
6. Writer drafts content.
7. Reviewer checks usefulness, safety, and clarity.
8. Final Verifier checks consistency and open questions.
9. Human Approval Gate accepts or redirects.
