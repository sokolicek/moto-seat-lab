# Countries And Localization

## Localization Strategy

Start with one country and one language. Avoid translating everything too early.

Pilot:

- Country: Germany
- Language: German
- Working content language: Slovak
- Optional support language later: English

## Planned Country Paths

```text

```

## Why Country Pages Matter

Each country differs in:

- popular motorcycle models,
- available retailers,
- shipping,
- language,
- climate,
- affiliate programs,
- workshop availability,
- product prices,
- legal expectations,
- riding habits.

## Germany First

Germany is a good pilot because:

- large motorcycle market,
- strong motorcycle accessory retail sector,
- many riders search in German,
- Amazon/Louis/POLO/eBay-style sources are relevant,
- touring and sport-touring content has strong potential.

## Research Needed Per Country

- most registered motorcycles or leading categories,
- top accessory retailers,
- common affiliate programs,
- model-specific forums,
- climate considerations,
- typical rider use cases,
- local terminology for seat, gel pad, seat cover, upholstery.

## Translation Model

Do not simply machine-translate. Create localized versions:

- German: direct and practical.
- Slovak: explanatory and personal.
- English: international overview.
- French/Italian/Polish/Hungarian: later, after content model stabilizes.
