# Affiliate Strategy

## Goal

Earn small commissions from helpful product recommendations without making the site feel like spam.

The site should first solve a real rider problem. Affiliate links should support the content, not replace it.

## Disclosure

Every page with affiliate links should include a clear disclosure:

```text
Some links may be affiliate links. If you buy through them, this site may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.
```

## Recommendation Rules

Do:

- recommend categories before specific products,
- explain why a product type helps,
- include price range,
- mention limitations,
- show alternatives.

Do not:

- claim a product solves all pain,
- hide affiliate links,
- recommend unsafe electrical work casually,
- rank products without testing or credible sources.

## Content Blocks

Future reusable blocks:


## Monetization Roadmap

1. Build useful content.
2. Add disclosure.
3. Add category-level buying guides.
4. Join affiliate programs.
5. Add product cards.
6. Track what content actually helps riders.
