# Germany Country Guide

Status: Draft concept

## Why Germany First

Germany is a strong first market for a motorcycle seat comfort site because:

- it has a large motorcycle community,
- German riders search in German for model-specific advice,
- motorcycle touring is common,
- accessory retail is well developed,
- affiliate opportunities are realistic.
