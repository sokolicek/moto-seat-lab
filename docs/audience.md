# Audience

## Primary Audiences

### 1. DIY Motorcycle Enthusiasts

People who enjoy modifying their motorcycle and want a practical guide before cutting foam or buying materials.

Needs:

- material choices,
- tool lists,
- step-by-step planning,
- mistakes to avoid,
- budget ranges,
- safety warnings.

### 2. Uncomfortable Riders

People who like their motorcycle but cannot sit comfortably for more than 30-90 minutes.

Needs:

- simple explanations,
- fast low-cost solutions,
- model-specific advice,
- comparison of gel, mesh, air, foam, and full seat replacement.

### 3. Touring Riders

People who ride long distances or multi-day trips.

Needs:

- long-distance comfort,
- heat and sweat management,
- pressure distribution,
- passenger comfort,
- waterproofing,
- durability.

### 4. Model-Specific Searchers

People searching for a specific bike model plus seat comfort, such as:

```text
Suzuki GSX-S1000GX Sitzbank Komfort
GSX-S1000GX seat upgrade
GSX-S1000GX gel seat
```

Needs:

- model overview,
- common comfort problems,
- compatible product categories,
- DIY notes,
- local buying options.

## Secondary Audiences

- motorcycle upholsterers,
- small workshops,
- motorcycle accessory shops,
- riders comparing professional vs DIY work,
- affiliate buyers looking for recommended materials/tools.
