# Seat Comparison Concept

## Goal

Allow riders to compare:

- stock seat,
- OEM comfort seat,
- aftermarket seats,
- professional custom seat,
- DIY modification plan,
- add-on overlays.

## Comparison Dimensions

```text
Seat height
Seat width
Seat slope
Foam support
Top comfort layer
Heat behavior
Rain behavior
Passenger comfort
Reversibility
DIY difficulty
Price
Availability
Warranty impact
Confidence level
```

## Example Comparison Table

| Option | Cost | Comfort Potential | Seat Height Impact | DIY Risk | Reversible | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| OEM stock | already owned | baseline | baseline | none | yes | starting point |
| 3D mesh cover | low | low-medium | small increase | low | yes | good for heat |
| Air cushion | low-medium | medium | small-medium increase | low | yes | can feel unstable |
| Gel insert | medium | medium | depends | medium | no/partial | can retain heat |
| Professional rebuild | high | high | configurable | low for rider | no/partial | best for touring |
| Aftermarket seat | high | medium-high | varies | low | yes | fitment must be verified |
| Full DIY build | variable | high if done well | configurable | high | no/partial | needs skill |

For each brand/model:

- compatible motorcycles,
- price range,
- country availability,
- return policy,
- heating option,
- passenger option,
- source URL,
- data confidence.

## Stock Seat Baseline

For each motorcycle, store:

- stock seat height,
- stock shape notes,
- known rider complaints,
- photos if available,
- measured dimensions if owner provides them,
- manufacturer specs.

## DIY Plan Comparison

A DIY plan should be compared against stock seat by:

- added/removed height,
- expected width/support change,
- material stack,
- cost,
- skill,
- reversibility,
- risk.

## Recommendation Output

Example:

```text
For your profile, a thick gel pad may raise the seat too much.
Try 3D mesh or air cushion first, then consider professional foam reshaping.
Confidence: medium, because exact seat width is missing.
```
